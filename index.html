Featured Projects

Nutrisolutions
NutriSolutions is a role-based nutrition management platform designed for clients, nutritionists, and administrators, each with their own dedicated interface and permissions. It allows clients to browse and save recipes with advanced filters and schedule appointments through an integrated calendar. Nutritionists can manage their availability, create personalized meal plans, and monitor consultation history with patients. Administrators are provided tools to oversee nutritionist accounts and verify their professional credentials.
Angular Flutter NestJS
E-Commerce Platform
SmartGros is a mobile-commerce platform connecting wholesalers and retailers. I began as part of a small team and now serve as the sole developer in charge of maintaining and expanding the mobile app frontend. I built the user interface, integrated push notifications, and contributed to the launch of the initial production version, which has over 100 downloads and incorporates early user feedback. The app supports internationalization in Arabic, French, and English (with Arabic currently live), and I’m currently leading the development of future releases that introduce features like product promotions, voice-based complaint submission, and post-order rating to improve engagement and user satisfaction.
